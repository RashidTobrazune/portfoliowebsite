Netflix Data
Cleaning and Analysis

Netflix is a popular streaming service that offers a vast catalog of movies, TV shows, and original contents.The data consist of contents added to Netflix approximately from 1960 to 2021. I retreived the dataset free from an online data community platform kaggle. This dataset was cleaned with MySQL and visualized with Power BI . The purpose of this dataset was to test my data cleaning and visualization skills.

Goal

The goal was to clean and visualize the dataset to provide relevant insights, asking the right questions and analyzing the data for better decision-making.

Results

The report provides actionable recommendations and next steps, supported by solid reasoning. It enables informed decision-making, and process improvement.

Skill Utilization

This project demonstrates a robust skill utilization, where SQL was used for data preparation and transformation, while Power BI was employed for data visualization and deriving meaningful insights. The seamless integration of these skills contributed to a comprehensive and actionable data analysis for this dataset.

Case Procedure


1. Data Cleaning

I performed several important data preparation and transformation steps when analyzing this Netflix dataset using SQL commands with MySQL.
Let's break down some part of what I did:

a. Treated Duplicates: Duplicates in the data could have skewed the analysis. I used 'HAVING' and 'COUNT' statements
to identify and remove duplicate rows based on one or more columns.

b. Treated Nulls: Handling null values is crucial. I used the 'IS NULL' and 'FILTER' statement to identify
and potentially replace null values in the dataset.

c. Populated Missing Rows: I created and populated missing rows to ensure the dataset is complete and accurate.
I used some statements like 'UPDATE', 'SET, 'WHERE' to insert missing data, replacing unambiguous values with 'Not given'.
It helped me assessed the quality of the dataset by checking for inconsistencies, data entry errors that might affect the integrity of the data.

2. Data Exploration and Analyzing

a. Using COUNTROWS and FILTER in DAX: In Power BI, I used the basic DAX language to create custom calculations and measures. I used 'COUNTROWS' function to count the number of rows in a table or a table expression, and 'FILTER' for filtering data based on specified conditions. I performed more calculations and filter data as needed for the analysis.

It is important to note that a typical data analysis process involves several other steps, such as data exploration, data visualization, and deriving insights. I applied transformations, such as scaling numerical features, and creating new measures based on domain knowledge.

3. Data Visualiztion:

In Power BI, I created graphical representations of the dataset to make it more accessible, reveal patterns, and communicate the findings effectively. I used line charts, bar charts, Map, tree map, and pie chart.

Asking Right Questions:

Analyzing a Netflix dataset involves asking various questions to extract valuable insights.
Here are some key questions I asked myself to guide my analysis:
What are the most prevalent genres and categories of content on Netflix?
Which directors or producers have the most content available on the platform?
What is the distribution of content based on release year?
Can we identify target demographics for specific types of content?
Demographics with highest movies on netflix.
Which viewer ratings has the highest content or popularity scores?
Are there specific genres that tend to lower content?

5. Ratings of Content(Movie and Tv show)

Ratings are a crucial system used to gauge the appropriateness of a motion picture for specific audiences, taking into account its content. In this chart, we highlight the highest-rated content on Netflix. A noteworthy observation is that the majority of content on Netflix carries a TV-MA rating. In the United States, the TV-MA rating, as defined by the TV Parental Guidelines, indicates content intended for Mature Audiences. It signifies that the material may be unsuitable for children under 17, containing elements such as strong language, explicit content, and intense violence. This rating serves as a vital indicator for viewers seeking content tailored to more mature sensibilities.

6. Release years with highest Contents

This chart illustrates the years in which Netflix released the highest number of movies and TV shows. We've specifically focused on the top 10 release years or production years. What's evident is that from 2012 to 2018, Netflix consistently added a substantial amount of recent content, ensuring that the quantity of recent releases per year outpaced older releases. However, in 2019 and the later years, there was a noticeable decline. While this decline might be attributed to the impact of Covid-19, further analysis would be required to confirm this hypothesis.
